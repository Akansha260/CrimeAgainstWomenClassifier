Crimes Against Women in India: Data Analysis Project

This project aims to provide a comprehensive analysis of crimes against women in India. By exploring this data, visitors can gain insights into the prevalence and distribution of various crimes, understand the significance of the issue, and learn about the measures being taken to address it.

Empowering Change

Understanding the data is the first step towards addressing the challenges women face in our society. Join us in making a difference with informed action and analysis.

Highlights

  • Key crime categories analyzed: Rape, Domestic Violence, Dowry Deaths, and more.
  • Top insights: Madhya Pradesh has the highest reported rape cases.
